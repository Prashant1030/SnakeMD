How to Play

  • Goal: Eat red food to score +1. Every 5 foods spawns a green bonus worth +5 for 4 seconds.
  • Move: Arrow keys or on-screen controls. Avoid hitting yourself (or walls if enabled).
  • Levels: Speed increases every 100 points. Settings let you tweak theme, walls, and level.
  • Pause: Center button or Space key. After game over, center button restarts.

Settings

Reach 100 points to unlock the next level.
